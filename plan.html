Goals

Slammers Beach Volleyball website is intended to increase the amount of people playing vollyball at Slammers each week by 50%. This will happen by promoting to people what Slammers Beach Volleyball is all about and How fun and enjoyable it is to play vollyball with other people.

Success Evaluation

One of the simple ways of testing the success of the website would be to count how many more people are attending after the website hase been developed and published. This would show Slammers Beach Volleyball how peopel have join since the websites development. Another way for Slammers Beach Volleyball to see the success is to ask people how they found out about their place, on entering. this will show how many people have heard from other people and how many people have found out through the website.

Target Audience

The Target Audience for Slammers Beach Volleyball is younger audience age between 15 and 18, this is to try and get highschool kids to start playing while they are younger and continue coming when they grow up.
